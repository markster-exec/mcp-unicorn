# Jon Xu — Judge Profile, Investment Thesis, Portfolio

## Background

YC General Partner (promoted May 2025 from visiting partner). Co-founded and served as CTO of FutureAdvisor (YC S10), early robo-advisor acquired by BlackRock in 2015. Led product and engineering to build enterprise-grade robo-advisor platform for large financial institutions. CS degree from MIT.

## Recent YC Investments (Led/Involved)

Heavy emphasis on **agentic AI** in regulated, high-stakes, enterprise B2B contexts:

- **Manufact (YC S25)**: MCP SDK + Cloud infrastructure. (Actively promoting/judging their events.)
- **Fenrock AI (W26)**: AI agents for Financial Crime Compliance (FINCRIME).
- **FINNY AI (S25)**: AI OS for independent financial advisors. Publicly congratulated $17M Series A.
- **Motives (S25)**: AI agents running full consumer focus groups.
- **ZeroEval**: Auto-optimizer for AI agents.
- **Nuntius (S25)**: Making Models Follow Rules (AI compliance/safety).
- **Protent (W26)**: AI intelligence for mission-critical operations.
- **Maywood (W26)**: AI for M&A.
- **sitefire (W26)**: Marketing suite for the agentic web.
- **NOSO LABS (S25)**: AI agents for field technicians.
- **Mesmer**: "Cursor for CTOs."
- **GovDash**: AI-native gov contracting (praised $30M Series B).

## Investment Thesis

- **Reliable, production-grade AI agents** solving real manual workflows in hard domains (fintech, compliance, ops, devtools).
- Strong emphasis on **MCP** — one of the most visible YC supporters of the MCP ecosystem.
- **High-trust, scalable systems for regulated/high-stakes environments** — mirroring his FutureAdvisor-to-BlackRock journey.
- Technical depth + operator mindset: MIT-level engineering, product-building for trust/scalability.
- B2B/enterprise SaaS or vertical AI tools that "democratize" complex services or 10x productivity.

## How to Frame Pitch for Jon Xu

- **Lead with operator-to-operator alignment**: "Like scaling FutureAdvisor from consumer to BlackRock's enterprise platform..."
- **Emphasize trust, compliance, enterprise readiness**: Auditability, safety, scalability, production reliability.
- **Tie explicitly to MCP/agentic wave**: "Built on the MCP standard which you've backed via Manufact..."
- **Show technical depth + B2B path**: Engineering rigor, early enterprise traction, clear go-to-market.
- **Reference his portfolio**: "Inspired by Fenrock (fin-crime agents) or FINNY (wealth AI) or Manufact (MCP infra)..."
- **Founder energy**: Technical, data-driven, community-oriented.

He responds to formidable technical teams solving painful, real-world problems with trustworthy AI that scales.
