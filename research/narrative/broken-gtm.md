# The Broken GTM Stack — Sourced Narrative

## Key Facts

### CEO Departures Timeline

1. **Cognism — James Isilay stepped down (Feb 2025)**

   - Co-founder, stepped aside to "pursue his next entrepreneurial endeavor"
   - Pete Daffern (former non-exec Chair) appointed Interim CEO
   - Search for permanent successor underway
   - [Source: BusinessCloud](https://businesscloud.co.uk/news/firm-with-100m-backing-reveals-leadership-change/)
   - [Source: Cognism Newsroom](https://www.cognism.com/newsroom/cognism-announces-leadership-change)

2. **6sense — Jason Zintak stepped down (Sep 9, 2025)**

   - Joined as CEO in 2017, scaled revenue from $5M to $250M+ over 8 years
   - Transitioned to Chairman of the Board
   - Chris Ball (ex-Instructure, Adobe, SAP) appointed new CEO
   - [Source: 6sense Newsroom](https://6sense.com/newsroom/6sense-welcomes-chris-ball-as-ceo/)
   - [Source: BusinessWire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20250909561976/en/6sense-Welcomes-Chris-Ball-as-CEO-Jason-Zintak-Continues-as-Chairman-of-the-Board)

3. **Outreach — Manny Medina stepped down (2024)**

   - Founded company a decade prior
   - Replaced by Abhijit Mitra (president of product and technology)
   - Medina launched new startup "Paid" (AI agent billing)
   - [Source: GeekWire](https://www.geekwire.com/2024/outreach-ceo-manny-medina-steps-down/)

4. **Clari + Salesloft — Merger completed (Dec 3, 2025)**
   - Merger announced Aug 7, 2025
   - Neither original CEO leads: Steve Cox (ex-Employ) appointed CEO
   - Salesloft already had Interim CEO (Patrick Nichols) at announcement
   - Combined: $10T in revenue under management
   - [Source: Salesloft Newsroom](https://www.salesloft.com/company/newsroom/clari-salesloft-merger)
   - [Source: BusinessWire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20251203184266/en/)

### 6sense & Intent Data Skepticism

- 6sense published "The Hard Facts About Intent Data" — essentially admitting problems with their own core product
- Key criticism: account-level only — "Until someone fills out a form, all you're getting are account-based insights. You're throwing darts at the wall."
- Data accuracy: multiple users report inaccurate contact data, "black box" approach
- Aggressive pricing: reports of 3x price increases alienating customers
- Commoditization: Clearbit (now HubSpot), others offering similar data at lower cost
- [Source: 6sense Events](https://6sense.com/events-webinars/hard-facts-about-intent-data/)

## Analysis

### What This Means for DealPulse

The timing is perfect. The entire GTM stack is in crisis:

1. **Leadership exodus** — When 4+ CEOs leave billion-dollar companies in the same category within 12 months, the category is broken, not just individual companies.

2. **Financial collapse** — ZoomInfo lost 90%+ of its peak value. The market is saying "this business model doesn't work."

3. **Self-admission** — 6sense's own content questions intent data validity. When the market leader admits their product doesn't work, the category is dead.

4. **Defensive consolidation** — Clari + Salesloft merger = two weakened players combining to survive, not to innovate. Neither original CEO stayed.

5. **Market still growing** — Despite all this chaos, the underlying market ($4.5B+) is growing at 11% CAGR. The NEED exists. The current SOLUTIONS don't work.

### DealPulse's positioning

"We're not iterating on the broken model. We're replacing it. Person-level intelligence (not company-level noise) + real-time financial context (not backward-looking data) + chat surface (not another SaaS tab)."
