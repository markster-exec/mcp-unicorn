# Anti-Handoff Sales Trend — Single-Thread Selling & Buyer Experience

## Buyer Frustration with Handoffs

- **70% of B2B buyers** say they worked with "so many different people on the supplier side that they weren't sure who everyone was" (SBI, 2024).
- **71% of buyers** describe their experience with supplier reps as "frustrating" (SBI, 2024).
- **61% of B2B buyers** prefer an overall rep-free buying experience (Gartner Sales Survey, June 2025).
- **73% actively avoid** suppliers sending irrelevant outreach (Gartner 2025).
- **69% report inconsistencies** between what sales reps say and website content (Gartner 2025).
- Buyers spend only ~17% of total buying time with ALL suppliers combined (Gartner).

## Full-Cycle Reps vs SDR/AE Split (Trend Back to Full-Cycle)

- **46% of SaaS and tech companies** are returning to a full-cycle sales model (Ebsta 2025 GTM Benchmarks).
- Full-cycle reduces sales cycle length by **20%** (McKinsey).
- Full-cycle drives **38% higher win rates** (Forrester).
- **52% of new revenue** now comes from existing accounts (Ebsta 2025) — ownership throughout the journey is critical.

## Deal Loss & Churn from Handoffs

- **86% of B2B purchases stall** during the process (Forrester 2024/2026).
- **81% of buyers** are dissatisfied with the provider they ultimately choose (Forrester).
- **60% of buyers** feel post-purchase regret within the first year due to broken transitions (Opine 2025).
- **23% of all customer churn** is caused by poor onboarding (tied to weak sales handoff).
- Strong Sales-CS alignment yields **36% higher retention** and **38% higher win rates**.

## Founder-Seller Advantage

- Founders bring unfair advantages: authentic vision, deepest product knowledge, passion, high-stakes conversations.
- Early revenue (0-$5M ARR) is almost always founder-led.
- Direct feedback loops → faster iteration. Many founders close deals SDR/AE teams couldn't.

## How This Supports "One AI Companion" Positioning

- Buyers exhausted by multiple humans, inconsistencies (69%), confusion over "who's who" (70%).
- AI companion = ultimate single-thread solution: always the same "person" across discovery → demo → negotiation → onboarding → support.
- Zero handoff loss — full context retained, no repetition, no inconsistencies.
- 24/7 availability + personalized guidance.
- Mirrors the full-cycle/anti-handoff trend while scaling infinitely.
