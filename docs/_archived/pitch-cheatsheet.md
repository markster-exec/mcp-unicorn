## Anti-Handoff / Buyer Pain

- **70%** of B2B buyers can't keep track of who they're talking to (SBI 2024)
- **71%** describe supplier experience as "frustrating" (SBI 2024)
- **61%** prefer rep-free buying (Gartner 2025)
- **86%** of B2B purchases stall mid-process (Forrester)
- **60%** post-purchase regret from broken transitions (Opine 2025)
- **46%** of SaaS companies returning to full-cycle model (Ebsta 2025)
- Buyers spend only **17%** of buying time with ALL suppliers combined (Gartner)

## Financial Context Gap

- **50-80%** of negotiated discounts are unnecessary (Professional Pricing Society, 25+ yrs data)
- **4-20%** revenue leakage from discounts annually (BCG 2020)
- **49%** of sales leaders saw more deals rejected by finance in 2025 (Creditsafe)
- **71%** of CFOs regretted approving high-risk deals (Creditsafe)
- 10% discount on 40% margin = need **33% more volume** to break even
- 20% discount = **100% more** volume. 30% = **300% more**.

- Only **24%** report exceptional ROI from intent tools
- Company-level triggers cause **36+ vendor outreaches in 2 weeks**
