## Puzzle's Product & Framework

- **"Autonomous Accounting"** — Sasha's framework with Levels 0-5 (like self-driving car levels):
  - Level 0: Manual bookkeeping
  - Level 1: Basic automation (bank feeds)
  - Level 2: Smart categorization
  - Level 3: Real-time financial intelligence
  - Level 4: Predictive anomaly detection
  - Level 5: Fully autonomous accounting
- **Key metrics/terminology** in their API:
  - `available_cash_balance`
  - `burn_multiple`
  - `spotlight_anomalies`
  - Revenue recognition, runway calculations
