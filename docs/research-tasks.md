## Key Findings Summary

### Market

- $1T+ SaaS market cap wiped (Feb 2026). HubSpot -70%, ZoomInfo -49%.
- 5 CEO departures in GTM stack (Cognism, 6sense, Outreach, Clari, Salesloft).
- 95-5 Rule: Only 5% of B2B buyers in-market per quarter. Company-level intent = broken math.
- 50-80% of negotiated discounts unnecessary (Professional Pricing Society).

### Anti-Handoff Trend

- 70% of B2B buyers can't track supplier contacts (SBI 2024).
- 46% of SaaS companies returning to full-cycle sales model (Ebsta 2025).
- 86% of purchases stall mid-process (Forrester).
- 61% prefer rep-free experience (Gartner 2025).

### Competitive

- **Vendis.ai** (Greylock): Closest — AI-native sales companion. Private beta. No financial modeling.
- **People.ai**: Announced MCP integration Feb 18 (3 days ago). Enterprise-only revenue intel.
- **No one** owns polished end-to-end in-chat sales companion with financial context.
- Confirmed greenfield for sales + finance in MCP.

### Sponsors

- **Anthropic** explicitly showcases sales/outreach MCP Apps (Clay = launch partner).
- **Both Anthropic + OpenAI** say "apps live inside chat, not separate visits."
- **WorkOS**: 1M MAUs free, RBAC in JWTs, FGA. Grinich thesis = MCP + AI auth.
- **Cloudflare**: Workers AI 10k Neurons/day free, AI Gateway for caching.
- **Puzzle**: No free API tier. Mock with exact terminology.

### Hackathon

- YC interview = **1st place / best team ONLY**.
- 50-150 participants, heavy on engineers. Less saturated: vertical apps with polished UX.
- Pre-seeded data is fine (no pre-built projects rule only).
- Manufact Cloud Hobby tier: 5 deploys, 500k req/mo FREE.
- Jon Xu thesis: agentic AI in regulated B2B. Portfolio: Fenrock, FINNY, Manufact, Maywood.

### YC Conversion

- Standard 10-min Zoom interview. Probes business viability, not just tech.
- April (YC S25): Won hackathon → 150 signups in 7 days → $10 early-access → got in.
- Key: "Ship over polish. Charge early." Treat days between as real interview prep.
